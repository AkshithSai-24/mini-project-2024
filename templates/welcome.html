ABOUT

Our platform combines with the expertise of medical professionals to help you assess and understand your heart health risks. We are dedicated to delivering accurate, reliable, and accessible healthcare insights.

Using the latest in machine learning, our heart disease prediction tool provides a seamless and user-friendly experience.This mini project aims to develop a machine learning model capable of accurately predicting the likelihood of heart disease based on various patient attributes. By leveraging techniques such as data preprocessing, feature engineering, model selection, and evaluation, the project seeks to create a valuable tool for healthcare professionals in early diagnosis, risk assessment, and personalized treatment planning.The model's potential impact includes improved patient outcomes, optimized resource allocation, and advancements in medical research.
